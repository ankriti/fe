ANKRITI Seed's VISION

Vision:
Ankriti’s Vision can be outlined as “VERVE meaning Vigor and Sprit”
V: Value Creation through high quality solutions to existing problems.
E: Empowering customers financially and academically.
R: Reinvigorating the economy of the country.
V: Valiantly work towards the company’s goals and mission.
E: Exploring new solutions and new opportunities continually.


Domain of Expertise

ANKRITI’s expertise lies in its knowledge base of the two major integrating aspects of its products: IT and Agriculture 

Core team comes with a fresh approach to how the agriculture sector has been functioning currently and how it could be done better. Their conjoined mission is to enable the Indian farmers to be successful agronomists and entrepreneurs –providing them effective tools for cultivation through products and services. Thus, catering to the current needs of the farmers and facilitate innovative ideas through incubation centers throughout the country as a future project.

While the key advisors, including scientists, and farm advisors ensure the quality of products is top-notch, the core team ensures new innovative approaches are fostered, tested, implemented and shared for business growth.

The company has its core values outlined in their tagline ‘YOUR TRUST, OUR INSPIRATION’ with its key focus on QUALITY in everything that goes on within the company and its external collaborations.

Mission:

Ankriti is on a mission to empower and connect Farmers nationwide. By providing quality products, personalized crop management services, and Artificial intelligence integrated machinery we are pioneering a faster, leaner and more innovation-centric approach to agricultural practices and services. Our vision is to make farming a sustainable and lucrative business nationwide through technological intervention and value creation.
